# Abstract Template

## Abstract

Write the abstract after completing the full report. Keep it concise, usually 150-250 words, and summarize the whole study in one paragraph.

Include:

- Research purpose
- Main research question
- Method and sample
- Key descriptive findings
- Welch t-test result
- Main recommendation

## Example Structure

This study examined how KIU undergraduate students perceive the effectiveness of KIU's internship support services in helping them find, prepare for, and apply to internships. A cross-sectional survey was conducted using Google Forms, and [number] consenting responses were analyzed using descriptive statistics and a Welch independent samples t-test. The study measured awareness, usage, satisfaction among support users, reasons for non-use, perceived internship readiness, and improvement priorities. The findings showed that [briefly state main descriptive result]. Students who used internship support services reported [higher/similar/lower] readiness scores than students who did not use the services, and the difference was [statistically significant/not statistically significant]. The results suggest that KIU internship support services are [overall interpretation], but improvements are needed in [main weakness areas]. The study recommends [main recommendation], [second recommendation], and [third recommendation] to strengthen undergraduate internship readiness.

## Final Check

Before submitting, make sure the abstract does not introduce new information that is not discussed in the report. Use `analysis/outputs/analysis-summary.md` after running the script to fill in the numerical findings.
