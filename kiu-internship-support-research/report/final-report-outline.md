# Final Report Outline

## Title Page

Include:

- Project title
- Course name
- Team member names
- Instructor name
- Submission date

## Abstract

Write this section last. Summarize the purpose, method, sample, main findings, and recommendations in about 150-250 words.

Example opening:

> This study examined how KIU undergraduate students perceive the effectiveness of KIU's internship support services in helping them find, prepare for, and apply to internships.

## 1. Introduction

Introduce the topic, explain why internships matter, describe the local problem, and present the research question and hypotheses.

Suggested subsections:

- Background
- Problem statement
- Research question
- Hypotheses
- Significance of the study

## 2. Literature Review

Organize the literature review by themes rather than by article. Connect previous research to this project's variables.

Suggested themes:

- Internships and employability
- University career support services
- Awareness and usage of services
- Student satisfaction and perceived usefulness
- Internship readiness

## 3. Methodology

Describe exactly how the study was conducted.

Suggested subsections:

- Research design
- Population and sample
- Instrument
- Variables and measures
- Data collection procedure
- Data analysis procedure
- Ethical considerations
- Limitations

## 4. Results

Present findings clearly without too much interpretation.

Suggested subsections:

- Sample characteristics
- Awareness and usage
- Service satisfaction and usefulness
- Internship readiness
- Readiness comparison and t-test
- Open-ended response themes

## 5. Discussion

Interpret the results in relation to the research question, hypotheses, and literature review. Explain what the findings suggest about KIU internship support services.

## 6. Conclusion and Recommendations

Summarize the main answer to the research question and provide practical recommendations for improving internship support.

## References

Use the citation style required by the instructor. Store BibTeX entries in `references.bib` if using reference management software.

## Appendices

Include:

- Appendix A: Survey questionnaire
- Appendix B: Consent form
- Appendix C: Extra tables
