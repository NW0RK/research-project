# Literature Search Strategy

## Purpose

The literature review should explain what previous research says about university career services, internship readiness, employability skills, student satisfaction, and the role of institutional support in internship access.

## Databases and Search Platforms

Use a mix of academic and practical sources:

- Google Scholar
- ResearchGate, only when full text is available and credible
- University library databases, if available
- ERIC
- Scopus or Web of Science, if accessible
- Official reports from universities, education ministries, or employability organizations

## Search Keywords

Use combinations of the following terms:

- university career services effectiveness
- internship support services undergraduate students
- student employability and career readiness
- internship readiness survey
- career development services student satisfaction
- employability skills higher education
- university-industry collaboration internships
- career guidance and internship preparation
- student awareness of career services

## Inclusion Criteria

Include sources that:

- Focus on undergraduate students, internships, career services, employability, or career readiness
- Are published in academic journals, conference proceedings, books, or credible institutional reports
- Provide findings relevant to awareness, usage, satisfaction, readiness, or service improvement
- Are preferably published within the last 10 years, unless they are foundational sources

## Exclusion Criteria

Exclude sources that:

- Are not related to higher education or student career development
- Have unclear authorship or weak credibility
- Focus only on graduate employment without discussing preparation or support
- Are opinion pieces without evidence

## Suggested Literature Review Themes

1. Importance of internships for undergraduate employability
2. Role of university career and internship support services
3. Student awareness and access barriers
4. Service quality and student satisfaction
5. Internship readiness and confidence
6. Recommendations for improving internship support

## Practical Tip

Use the source matrix file to summarize each article immediately after reading it. This prevents the team from losing useful ideas and makes the literature review easier to write.
