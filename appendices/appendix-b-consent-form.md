# Appendix B: Consent Form

## Study Title

Evaluating the Effectiveness of KIU's Internship Support Services for Undergraduate Students

## Purpose of the Study

The purpose of this study is to understand how KIU undergraduate students perceive KIU's internship support services. The study focuses on awareness, usage, satisfaction, perceived usefulness, internship readiness, weaknesses, and improvement suggestions.

## Participation

Participation is voluntary. You may choose not to participate. If you begin the survey, you may stop before submitting your response.

## Time Required

The survey takes approximately 5–7 minutes to complete.

## Risks and Benefits

There are no expected major risks. Some questions ask about your opinions of university support services. The study may benefit the KIU community by identifying ways to improve internship support for students.

## Privacy

The survey does not ask for your name, student ID, email address, phone number, or other direct identifiers, and no such information is recorded in the exported data. Please do not include identifying details in open-ended responses.

## Data Use

Responses will be used only for academic purposes in the Basic Research Methods and Tools course. Results will be reported in summary form.

## Consent Statement

By selecting "Yes, I agree to participate" in the survey, you confirm that:

- You understand the purpose of the study.
- Your participation is voluntary.
- Your responses may be used for academic analysis and reporting.
- You understand that results will be reported in aggregate form.
