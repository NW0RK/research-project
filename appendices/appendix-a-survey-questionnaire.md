# Appendix A: Survey Questionnaire

## Student Perceptions of KIU Internship Support Services

This appendix contains the questionnaire to be entered into Google Forms. The form should use section branching so that respondents answer only the questions relevant to their support-service usage.

## Google Forms Branching

1. Consent is required.
2. If the respondent does not consent, send them to a final thank-you section.
3. If the respondent consents, continue to the main survey.
4. If the respondent has used KIU internship support services, show the support-use details and service-evaluation items.
5. If the respondent has not used KIU internship support services, skip the service-evaluation items and ask the non-use reason.
6. All consenting respondents answer internship readiness, improvement priority, and open-ended questions.
