# Appendix A: Survey Questionnaire

## Student Perceptions of KIU Internship Support Services

This appendix reproduces the questionnaire as it was deployed in Google Forms. The form used section branching so that respondents answered only the questions relevant to their support-service usage. All Likert items used a 1–5 scale where 1 = Strongly disagree and 5 = Strongly agree.

## Google Forms Branching

1. Consent was required.
2. Respondents who did not consent were sent to a final thank-you section and the form was submitted.
3. Respondents who consented continued to the main survey.
4. Respondents who had used KIU internship support services saw the support-use details and service-evaluation items (Section 5A).
5. Respondents who had not used the services skipped the service-evaluation items and answered the non-use reason (Section 5B).
6. All consenting respondents answered the internship readiness, improvement priority, and open-ended questions.

Google Forms was configured to accept only one response per account, preventing duplicate submissions. No email addresses or other identifiers were recorded in the exported data.
